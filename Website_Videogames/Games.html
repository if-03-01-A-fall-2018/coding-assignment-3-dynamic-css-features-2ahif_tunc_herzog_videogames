Games

Tetris

You control the bricks falling down. Fit them in a row to get points.

Use the arrow buttons.

Pacman

You have to stear the yellow ball (pacman) and collect all the points in the maze.

The ghosts are trying to eat you, but you can use a powerup to turn the game around for a while. Use the arrow buttons.

Pong

You have to stear the left rectangle (ping) to win against the A.I. (pong).

Use the up/down arrows or w/s. Winner is who gets the most points by shooting the ball into the enemy's field.
